Day 01

    The Foundations

    Nutrition

    Anabolic Window

    Consume 30-50g protein within 2 hours of lifting to maximize muscle protein synthesis.

    Anti-Bloat Protocol

    Sodium < 2g/day. High Potassium (Bananas/Potatoes) to flush water retention.

    Vascularity

    Lipid Oxidation (LISS)

    Incline Zone 2 Walking

    45 mins at 130bpm. This specific heart rate maximizes fat oxidation while keeping cortisol low. Elevated cortisol causes the body to hold facial water weight (moon face).

    Angiogenesis (HIIT)

    Capillary Branching Sprints

    15 mins (30s sprint / 30s rest). High-intensity work forces the body to create new micro-capillaries, leading to permanent vascularity in the forearms and delts.

    Plasma Expansion

    Intravascular Fullness

    Supplementing L-Citrulline Malate prior to cardio sessions increases Nitric Oxide, expanding the vein walls for a "thick" vascular look.

    Thermal Shock

    Vasoconstriction Cycling

    End every session with 3 mins of ice-cold water. This cycles blood from the skin to the core and back, "training" the skin to shrink-wrap the muscle.

    Derm Lab

    Tretinoin

    "Prescription-strength Vitamin A for skin cell turnover."

    Apply only at night. Starts with 0.025%. Reverses sun damage and clears acne.

    Dermastamping

    Micro-needling (0.5mm) once a week to induce collagen and thicken facial skin.

    Structure

    Mewing (Orthotropics)

    Tongue posture. Press your whole tongue against the roof of your mouth. Sharpens the jawline over time.

    Postural Correction

    "Chin Tucks" daily. Pull your head back until you have a double chin. Hold 5s. Fixes "Nerd Neck."

    Sleep Opt

    Mouth Taping

    Force nasal breathing at night. Prevents "mouth breather face" and dry lips. Sharpens jawline definition by reducing facial puffiness.

    Back Sleeping

    Sleep on your back with a silk pillowcase to prevent sleep wrinkles and asymmetrical facial compression.

    Hyper-Aesthetics

    Vocal Resonance Training

    Humming exercises to shift speaking voice from the throat to the "mask" (chest/nose). Increases perceived authority and testosterone signaling.

    Limbal Ring Enhancement

    Lutein-rich diet + hydration. Maximizing the contrast between the iris and the white (sclera) of the eye for a "youthful" and "predatory" gaze.
